UK Road Traffic Accident Visual Analysis

In the year 2015, road traffic accidents have resulted in an alarming figure of 186,130 casualties. The aim of this visual analysis is to understand what are the different factors contributing to those accidents, and the findings may then be used to reduce the number of casualties on roads.
